Transformation Houses

On this SITE will rise sacred spaces of disciplined formation — Transformation Houses established for the spiritual training, intellectual refinement, emotional mastery, and covenant alignment of the elect.

These houses shall not merely be buildings, but structured environments of awakening — where identity is restored, purpose clarified, character forged, and calling activated through guided study, structured obedience, community accountability, and practical mission deployment.

Within these centers, individuals will undergo layered transformation: mind renewed through truth, body strengthened through discipline, spirit aligned through prayer and reflection, and leadership cultivated through service and responsibility.

The Transformation Houses shall stand as training grounds for those who choose the narrow way — not for spectacle, but for formation; not for recognition, but for impact; not for comfort, but for consecration.

Here the elect are refined, prepared, and sent.
