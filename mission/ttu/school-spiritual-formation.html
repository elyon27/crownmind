School of Spiritual Formation

The goal is not performance but transformation: disciplined inner life, emotional maturity, prayer strength, and identity alignment under Scripture.

Certificate Paths (Phase I)

  • Certificate in Emotional Maturity & Spiritual Integration — stability, emotional stewardship, inner order
  • Certificate in Identity & Calling Discernment — vocation clarity, gifts, direction, obedience
  • Certificate in Prayer Discipline & Intercession — rhythm, structure, endurance, focus

Formation Practices

  • Daily Scripture + reflection rhythm
  • Repentance checkpoint (weekly)
  • Prayer assignments (structured)
  • Accountability markers (tracked)

Prototype Module Set (Suggested)

  • SF-01: Inner Alignment Under Scripture
  • SF-02: Emotional Stewardship & Discipline
  • SF-03: Prayer Rhythm (Daily / Weekly / Seasonal)
  • SF-04: Repentance, Repair, and Renewal
  • SF-05: Identity, Calling, and Obedient Direction
Phase I aim: measurable formation — not just learning. Modules require reflection work, practical obedience steps, and recorded outcomes.

Phase I Integration with Chapel

This school is designed to integrate naturally with Chapel rhythms. Students do not “switch modes” — they live the formation system daily.

  • Module reflection aligns with weekly prayer focus
  • Identity/calling modules pair with Scripture memorization
  • Emotional maturity modules pair with repentance checkpoints
