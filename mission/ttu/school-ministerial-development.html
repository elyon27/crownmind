School of Ministerial Development

Ministry is not improvisation — it is trained faithfulness. This school forms readiness through doctrine, discernment, pastoral foundations, and evangelism practice.

Certificate Paths (Phase I)

  • Certificate in Apologetics — clarity, defense of faith, response to objections
  • Certificate in Pastoral Foundations — shepherding, care, counsel, oversight
  • Certificate in Discipleship & Evangelism — message, method, follow-up, accountability

Required Formation Outcomes

  • Clear articulation of Gospel essentials
  • Ability to counsel with Scripture and wisdom
  • Discernment: truth vs. error vs. noise
  • Practical discipling: follow-through and formation rhythms

Phase I Delivery (How You Learn)

Each certificate is divided into modules. Modules are completed through lessons, reflection, and short assessments. Completion is verified through documented submission.

Prototype Module Set (Suggested)

  • MD-01: The Call to Shepherd
  • MD-02: Sound Doctrine & Guarding the Deposit
  • MD-03: Evangelism with Clarity and Courage
  • MD-04: Pastoral Care & Counseling Foundations
  • MD-05: Discernment & Spiritual Authority

Launch-Ready Actions (Phase I)

These are simple and operational even without an LMS:

  • Publish module pages using the TTU Module Template
  • Attach downloadable worksheets (PDF)
  • Collect submissions via email / form (Phase II upgrades to secure portal)
  • Registrar logs completion and issues certificates
