Admissions

Phase I admissions are simple, structured, and accountable. Students begin by choosing a certificate path and committing to disciplined completion.

Phase I Admission Steps

  1. Choose a certificate path
  2. Read TTU Identity + Charter
  3. Submit a short applicant statement
  4. Receive enrollment confirmation
  5. Begin Module 1
Phase I Standard: This is a formation path — not casual consumption. Certificates are issued only for verified completion.

Applicant Statement (Prototype)

In Phase I, this can be a simple email process or a form later. For now, provide instructions and a contact method.

  • Full Name
  • Chosen certificate path
  • Why you seek formation
  • Commitment statement (time & discipline)
