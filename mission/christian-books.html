Doctrinal Statement

CrownMind Mission Literature is written to honor Christ, uphold Scripture, and form faithful lives. Our instruction is anchored in the historic Christian confession and submits to the authority of the Word of God.

  • Scripture is inspired, authoritative, and sufficient for faith and obedience.
  • Christ is Lord and Savior — the center of redemption, discipleship, and truth.
  • Grace calls us to repentance, holiness, and persevering obedience.
  • The Church is the training ground for formation, service, and mission.
  • Formation is lived theology: truth practiced through disciplined habits.
Key Scriptures: Matthew 7:13–14 • Romans 12:1–2 • 2 Timothy 3:16–17 • John 14:6
